Ingredients:
 - 1/2 lb Dou Miao
 - 2 Garlic Cloves
 - 12 fl oz. Olive Oil

Directions:
 1. Heat pan with 9 fl oz Olive oil until oil is hot.
 
 2. Slice garlic cloves into thin pieces and put in oil when hot.
 
 3. Place separated Dou Miao into pan 30 seconds after garlic and stir thoroughly.
 Pour in remaining olive oil as necessary. Cook for 5-8 minutes or until leaves
 have cooked through and have shrunk a bit.
